Orange Star COs

Andy

Andy is an Orange Star CO whose lack of any strengths or weaknesses allows for more versatile play. As such, Andy is a good CO choice for beginners.

Power Bar
Strength None
Weakness None
Day-to-Day Effects
None. All of Andy's units have regular offense and defense.
Power: Hyper Repair
Restores 2 HP to all of his Units and increases their defense by 10% (And offense by 10% in Dual Strike).
Super Power: Hyper Upgrade
Restores 5 HP to all of his Units, increases firepower by 30%, defense by 10%, and movement by 1 space.

Max

Max is a direct-combat specialist, possesing significant firepower bonuses to ground, air, and naval direct-attack units. The added firepower is usually enough to overwhelm most other CO's directs and put armies on the defensive, especially on maps spacious enough for direct units to roam free.

This advantage is offset by range and firepower penalties for his indirects, usually putting Max into situations where he must gain a decisive advantage before the opposing army fully develops and pushes him back with superior indirects.

Power Bar
Strength Direct Combat
Weakness Indirect Combat
Day-to-Day Effects
All of Max's Direct Combat units have 20% increased offense, and all of his Indirect Combat units suffer -1 Range.
Power: Max Force
Firepower of all non-infantry Direct Combat is increased by 40%. All units gain a 10% defence bonus.
Super Power: Max Blast
Firepower of all non-infantry Direct Combat is increased by 60%. All units gain a 10% defence bonus.

Sami

Sami is a CO who specializes in Foot soldiers and capturing properties. Her increased capture speed will allow her to take contested properties more easily, and her high offensive boost for foot soldiers will ensure any enemy Infantry will take significant damage while she is capturing.

Power Bar
Strength Infantry, Transports
Weakness Direct Combat
Day-to-Day Effects
All of Sami's foot soldiers have 20% increased offense and capture properties 50% faster (rounded down). Direct combat vehicles have 10% decreased offense.
Power: Double Time
Increases movement range by one for all Infantry and Mechs by 1 space, and their firepower is raised by 60%. They also receive a 10% defence bonus. All other Units get a 10% boost in firepower and defence.
Super Power: Victory March
Increases movement range of Infantry and Mech units by 2 spaces. All foot soldiers gain a 90% increase in firepower and may capture in one turn, regardless of HP.
