Blue Moon Commanders

Olaf

Olaf is a Blue Moon CO who shines in snow, suffers in rain, and is otherwise average.

Being an all-around average CO, Olaf will play very similarly to Andy, with differences only taking hold as CO Powers begin to take effect.

Power Bar
Strength Snow Movement
Weakness Rain Movement
Day-to-Day Effects
Olaf's units are unaffected by snow, but are slowed in rain equivalently to how other COs are slowed in snow. See weather effects for details. In addition, snow also raises Olaf's units' firepower by 20%.
Power: Blizzard
Olaf changes the map's weather to Snow for 2 days (meaning it snows until his second turn after activating his power), and gains 20% increased offense and defense on the first day, and 10% on the second.
Super Power: Winter Fury
All enemy units lose 2 HP (but will not fall below 0.1 HP displayed as 1), Olaf changes the map's weather to Snow for 2 days, retaining the same benefits of the normal CO Power.

Grit

Grit is an indirect combat specialist, capable of outranging and outdamaging enemy indirects day-to-day. His direct units, while considerably weaker offensively, have good enough defense to defend his indirects, leading to situations where trying to attack a well-positioned clump of his indirects, infantry, and tanks is futile due to the heavy indirect counterattacks.

Power Bar
Strength Indirect Combat
Weakness Direct Combat
Day-to-Day Effects
All of Grit's Indirect Combat units have 20% increased offense and +1 Range, but all of his Direct Combat units have 20% decreased offense. Grit's foot soldiers are average.
Power: Snipe Attack
All of Grit's Indirect Combat units gain 60% increased offense (as opposed to the normal 20%), 10% increased defense and an additional +1 range. All other units gain 10% increased offense and defense.
Super Power: Super Snipe
All of Grit's Indirect Combat units gain 60% increased offense, 10% increased defense and an additional +2 range. All other units gain 10% increased offense and defense.

Colin

Colin is a who uses lowered unit costs (in exchange for lowered unit firepower) and cash-boosting powers to gain a large advantage in battles.

Power Bar
Strength Lowered Unit Cost
Weakness Lowered Attack
Day-to-Day Effects
All of Colin's units costs 20% less to build, but have 10% decreased offense.
Power: Gold Rush
Colin's current Funds are multiplied by 1.5. All of Colin's units gain 10% increased offense and defense.
Super Power: Power of Money
Colin uses money to increase weapon strength. Every 1000 Funds grants 3.33% additional firepower to all units. Regardless of funds, all of Colin's units gain 10% increased offense and defense as well. This power does not use up Colin's Funds.

Sasha

Sasha is a versatile all-around CO, whose Day-To-Day ability is viable on most any map that has a decent amount of properties. Even when keeping up with her opponent in property count, Sasha can build a funds advantage over time, allowing her to build higher-tier units more easily than her opponent.

Sasha's biggest weakness is her lack of any combat-related boosts. Even with the 10% increased offense/defense granted from her CO Powers, Sasha's units are middling at best, which may give her difficulty when pushing through defensive lines and closing out games.

Power Bar
Strength Increased Income
Weakness None
Day-to-Day Effects
Every property Sasha owns provide her with an additional 100 funds per day. Only properties that provide funds to begin with gain this bonus.
Power: Market Crash
All Enemy COs' Power Bars are drained by 10% for every 5000 funds she has. If Sasha has 50,000 Funds or more, all Enemy CO Power Bars are reduced to no charge. All of Sasha's units gain 10% increased offense and defense.
Super Power: War Bonds
Sasha gains funds equal to 50% of the financial damage her units deal to enemy units, as long as the power is active. For example, if Sasha destroys a Full HP Tank (which costs 7000), she gains 3500 funds. All of Sasha's units gain 10% increased offense and defense.
