Yellow Comet Commanders

Kanbei

Kanbei is a Yellow Comet CO whose units are more expensive and much stronger than regular units, with his CO Power boosts making them nigh untouchable.

Power Bar
Strength Stronger Offense/Defense
Weakness Higher Costs
Day-to-Day Effects
All Kanbei's units have a 20% increase to offence and defence, but they also cost 20% more to deploy.
Power: Morale Boost
Increases the offence of Kanbei's Units by 60%. They also get a defence bonus of 10%.
Super Power: Samurai Spirit
Increases the offence and defence of Kanbei's Units by 70%. Firepower doubles when inflicting damage in counter attacks.

Sonja

Sonja is a CO who relies on limiting opponent's information to force bad match ups. She is also unfortunately cursed with Bad Luck.

Fog of War is a necessity for Sonja as she is heavily underpowered without its presence..

Power Bar
Strength Increased Vision and Counterattacks
Weakness Bad Luck
Day-to-Day Effects
All of Sonja's units have +1 Vision in Fog of War. Regardless of Fog, Sonja hides her units' HP status from her enemies, who will instead only see a ? (Sonja herself and her allies can still see her HP normally). Sonja's units have 50% increased offense when counterattacking.
Power: Enhanced Vision
All of Sonja's units gain an additional +1 vision in fog, and can see into Forests and Reefs without having to be adjacent to them. All of Sonja's units gain 10% increased offense and defense.
Super Power: Counter Break
All of Sonja's units gain the same benefits as the normal CO Power. In addition, when attacked in direct combat, Sonja will always get the first strike for as long as the power is active. Note that the first strike damage is normal (not enhanced by the passive counterattack bonus).

Sensei

Sensei is a CO whose strength lies in overwhelming opponents with massive amounts of Footsoldiers and B-Copters.

His copters are super powerful, and his infantry are as well. His transports get +1 movement. As such he can beat any CO who cannot shield well against his swarms; on the flip side, he is countered by COs who can.

Power Bar
Strength Infantry, Copters and Transport
Weakness Land and Naval Vehicles
Day-to-Day Effects
Sensei's Foot Soldiers have +10% attack. Battle Copters have +50% attack. All naval units suffer a 10% attack penalty. Transport units have +1 movement.
Power: Copter Command
Increases the firepower of all Battle Copters by 80% and creates Infantry units with 9 HP at every allied city, ready to move.
Super Power: Airborne Assault
Increases the firepower of all Battle Copters by 80% and a Mech unit with 9 HP is created on every allied city, ready to move.

Grimm

Grimm is a CO who favors crushing foes with raw power, at the expense of defense.

His strong offensive boost and terrible defense vastly change the way in which the game is played. First strikes are much more important than in standard gameplay, and defensive terrain does much less to discourage attacks. Attacking a unit on an uncaptured city usually results in a win for the attacker, although captured cities still give a strong advantage to the defender due to the power of repair.

Power Bar
Strength High Offense
Weakness Low Defense
Day-to-Day Effects
Grimm's Units have 130% firepower, but only 80% defense.
Power: Knuckleduster
Increases Grimm's Units' firepower by 60% and defence by 10% (so his units now have 90% defense overall).
Super Power: Haymaker
Increases Grimm's Units' firepower by 90% and defence by 10%.
